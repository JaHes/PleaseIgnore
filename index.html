About TEST

TEST Alliance Please Ignore is an Eve Online alliance of like-minded corporations brought together with the idea of having fun and blowin' shit up in space. Dreddit is the founding corporation of TEST, born from the social news aggregation site Reddit.

Where We Live

Esoteria is located in the galactic South, as far south as you can go in Eve. The region is ripe with Sansha rats, many wonderful asteroids and high-end moons. Esoteria is also home to the memorial to Steve, the first titan killed in Eve Online. Historically Esoteria has been home to Stainwagon through its various evolutions. Traditionally this coalition has simply retreated to Stain, the nearby NPC region, to wait out invasions and then to return home as the invading forces collapse from the lack of adaptation to living in such a remote portion of space. After the Great Hellpurge of the East by the Honey Badger Coalition back in 2012 however, the region saw its traditional stability rocked by a handful of entities that rotated through in the absence of a strong Against All Authorities presence in Catch.

How To Join?

If your corporation is interested in joining TEST, please contact TEST Diplomacy. If you're an individual pilot interested in joining Dreddit, they're always recruiting as are many member corps (see below). Join in-game channel TEST Alliance Public for more information and talk to corp recruiters.
